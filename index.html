Let me introduce myself

Hi! My name is Markus, I'm 25 years old and live in Copenhagen. Right now I'm studying Multi Media Design at KEA (Københavns Erhversakademi) and I'm looking for a challenging but fun internship!

My main focus is Front End development and webdesign, but I have been taught in (almost) every aspect of communication and design so far. Some of my Front End skills are HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SASS and REACT and I'm looking forward to be expanding this list! I'm also comfortable with WordPress and could easily learn another CMS. I have plenty of experience with customer relations and customer service from previous jobs, and I'm a great listener.

I love meeting and talking to new and interesting people that I can learn from and be inspired by, but I also value being alone and getting to dive deep down in to my work or hobbies. One of my hobbies is photography and video production, and you can follow me here on Instagram to see some of my pictures. I've also studied Film- og tv-produktionsteknikker at NEXT CPH and you can see some of my projects here.

I'm both a coffee and a tea drinker, and can always go for a "fyraftensøl" - so feel free to contact me if you want to know more!
